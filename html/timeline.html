Project Timeline
In this project, you will complete different tasks step by step.

 Session 1
Watch a video about London and share what you already know.

Session 2
Learn vocabulary about London landmarks.

 Session 3
Learn about sustainable transport and create a mini-poster.

Session 4
Practice giving directions through role-play.

 Sessions 5–6
Research about London landmarks and eco-friendly transport.

 Session 7
Design your sustainable itinerary.

 Session 8
Present your project orally in English.

 Session 9
Complete a self-assessment and reflect on your learning.
