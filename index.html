Welcome to our project!

In this project, you are going to become a travel planner and explore one of the most famous cities in the world: London!

Your mission is to design a one-day trip around London in a sustainable (eco-friendly) way. You will work in teams, use English to communicate, and create your own digital travel guide.

Are you ready to discover London and help the planet at the same time?
Let’s go!
